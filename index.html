Sportcar History

Along with a massive growth of America's middle class, history of the modern sports-car begins directly after WW2. European vehicles found new homes after the war, brought to the United States by returning soldiers. This brought a new-found interest in smaller, lighter cars that made for spirited driving, particularly on tight, winding roads. British auto manufacturers were quick to capitalize on America's growing interest in sports cars. During 1946 to 1952, a handful of small U.S. manufacturers also jumped on board.
